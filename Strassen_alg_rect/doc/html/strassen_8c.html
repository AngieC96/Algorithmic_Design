Naive algorithm for matrix multiplication.

Parameters
CThe matrix where to put the result.
AThe first matrix of the multiplication.
BThe second matrix of the multiplication.
C_f_rowThe starting point on the rows of the sub-matrix of C.
C_f_colThe starting point on the columns of the sub-matrix of C.
A_f_rowThe starting point on the rows of the sub-matrix of A.
A_f_colThe starting point on the columns of the sub-matrix of A.
B_f_rowThe starting point on the rows of the sub-matrix of B.
B_f_colThe starting point on the columns of the sub-matrix of B.
nThe dimension of the matrices.

This function implements the naive algorithm for matrix multiplication between sub-matrixes. The result is placed in the sub-matrix C. The parameters *_f_row and *_f_col represents the first row and the first column, respectively, of the sub-matrix we want to deal with.
